## Production Tips

### 🎸 **Guitar Tones by Subgenre**

| Subgenre | Guitar Approach | Key Effect |
|----------|-----------------|------------|
| **Bedroom Pop** | Clean/minimal distortion | Warm tape saturation |
| **Jangly Indie** | Rickenbacker clean tone | Chorus + compression |
| **Shoegaze** | Heavily distorted layers | Tremolo + reverb |
| **Dream Pop** | Reverb-drenched clean | Shimmer reverb |
| **Folk Indie** | Fingerpicked acoustic | Natural room sound |
| **Lo-Fi Indie** | Fuzzy analog distortion | Tape saturation |

### 🎤 **Vocal Characteristics**

- **Bedroom Pop:** Intimate, close-mic'd, vulnerable, conversational
- **Indie Rock:** Natural, minimal processing, authentic character
- **Shoegaze:** Buried in mix, ethereal, textural element
- **Dream Pop:** Breathy, airy, reverb-heavy, romantic
- **Folk Indie:** Clear storytelling, emotional delivery, upfront
- **Lo-Fi Indie:** Raw, unpolished, authentic imperfections

### 🥁 **Drum Production**

- **Bedroom Pop:** Programmed or minimal live, lo-fi texture, tape compression
- **Indie Rock:** Natural kit, room mics, dynamic playing
- **Shoegaze:** Buried in guitar wash, reverb-heavy, textural
- **Dream Pop:** Soft drum machines or gentle live, spacious
- **Folk Indie:** Minimal acoustic, brushes, organic percussion
- **Lo-Fi Indie:** Vintage drum machines, cassette tape sound, compressed

### 📊 **Typical BPM Ranges**

- **Bedroom Pop:** 85-110 BPM (laid-back intimate)
- **Indie Rock:** 120-150 BPM (upbeat energetic)
- **Shoegaze:** 90-120 BPM (slow hypnotic)
- **Dream Pop:** 80-110 BPM (slow dreamy)
- **Folk Indie:** 70-100 BPM (storytelling pace)
- **Lo-Fi Indie:** 90-125 BPM (varies by style)

### 🎚️ **Production Aesthetics**

- **Bedroom Pop:** DIY home recording, tape warmth, intimate proximity
- **Indie Rock:** Natural room sound, minimal processing, live feel
- **Shoegaze:** Dense layering, pedal-heavy, wall of sound
- **Dream Pop:** Lush reverb, ethereal space, romantic atmosphere
- **Folk Indie:** Organic acoustics, natural dynamics, clear storytelling
- **Lo-Fi Indie:** Analog warmth, cassette tape, vinyl crackle, authentic imperfections
