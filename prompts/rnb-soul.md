## Production Tips

### 🎤 **Vocal Production by Era**

| Era/Style | Vocal Approach | Key Technique |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Modern Trap-Soul** | Auto-tune/pitch correction | Heavy reverb + delay |
| **Classic Soul** | Raw powerful delivery | Minimal processing, compression only |
| **Neo-Soul** | Natural with character | Light compression, warmth |
| **Quiet Storm** | Silky smooth | De-esser, gentle compression |
| **Alt-R&B** | Experimental textures | Heavy effects, layering |

### 🎹 **Signature Instruments**

- **Modern Trap-Soul:** 808 bass, atmospheric synth pads, sparse keys
- **Classic Soul:** Hammond organ, live strings, tambourine, Fender Rhodes
- **Neo-Soul:** Rhodes electric piano, live bass, jazz guitar, organic drums
- **Quiet Storm:** Lush strings, soft synth pads, gentle piano
- **Alt-R&B:** Experimental synths, electronic textures, glitchy elements

### 🥁 **Drum Approaches**

- **Trap-Soul:** Trap drums (808, snare, hi-hat rolls), half-time feel
- **Classic Soul:** Live drums, tambourine, handclaps, four-on-floor
- **Neo-Soul:** Live kit with hip-hop influence, loose groove
- **Quiet Storm:** Brushed drums, minimal percussion, soft touch
- **Alt-R&B:** Programmed + glitchy, unconventional rhythms

### 📊 **Typical BPM Ranges**

- **Modern Trap-Soul:** 65-80 BPM (slow intimate feel)
- **Classic Soul:** 100-125 BPM (upbeat groovy energy)
- **Neo-Soul:** 85-95 BPM (laid-back sophisticated)
- **Quiet Storm:** 60-75 BPM (slow romantic vibe)
- **Alt-R&B:** Variable 70-130 BPM (experimental approach)
- **Upbeat R&B:** 105-120 BPM (radio-friendly energy)

### 🎚️ **Mix/Master Characteristics**

- **Modern R&B:** Sub-bass prominence, reverb-heavy vocals, trap mix approach
- **Classic Soul:** Vintage warmth, analog tape saturation, balanced mix
- **Neo-Soul:** Organic warmth, jazz-influenced space, natural dynamics
- **Quiet Storm:** Smooth polish, no harsh frequencies, intimate proximity
- **Alt-R&B:** Experimental fx, unconventional panning, artistic choices
