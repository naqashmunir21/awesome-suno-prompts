## 💡 Pop Production Tips

### Vocal Production
- **Verse:** Dryer, more intimate vocals
- **Chorus:** Heavy reverb, doubled vocals, harmonies
- **Bridge:** Often stripped-back or heavily processed for contrast

### Structure Patterns
- **Radio Formula:** Intro (8s) → Verse → Pre-Chorus → Chorus → Verse → Pre-Chorus → Chorus → Bridge → Final Chorus
- **Modern Pop:** Often starts with chorus hook immediately
- **TikTok Optimized:** Hook within first 8 seconds

### Key Characteristics
- **Major keys** for upbeat/happy (C, G, D, A, F)
- **Minor keys** for emotional/moody (A minor, E minor, D minor)
- **BPM range** typically 100-140
