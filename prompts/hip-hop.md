## Production Tips

### 🎛️ **808 Bass Patterns by Subgenre**

| Subgenre | Bass Style | Slide Pattern | Notable Feature |
|----------|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Trap** | Long sustained | Gliding slides | Sub-bass focus |
| **Boom Bap** | Short punchy | No slides | Kick-focused |
| **Drill** | Rapid slides | Melodic motion | Dark menacing |
| **G-Funk** | Smooth sine | Subtle glides | Deep sub layer |
| **Emo Rap** | Melodic | Chord-based | Emotional tone |

### 🎤 **Vocal Delivery Styles**

- **Trap:** Auto-tuned, melodic, ad-libs frequent, reverb-heavy
- **Boom Bap:** Clear articulation, minimal effects, lyric-focused
- **Melodic Rap:** Singing-rapping hybrid, pitch correction, emotional
- **Drill:** Aggressive, rhythmic, minimal melody, intense delivery
- **Conscious:** Clear enunciation, storytelling pace, minimal processing
- **Old School:** Energetic, party vibe, call-and-response, group vocals

### 🥁 **Drum Programming**

- **Trap:** Rolling hi-hats (1/32 triplets), sparse kick/snare, 808 bass
- **Boom Bap:** Hard kick + snare (2 and 4), minimal hi-hats, vinyl feel
- **Drill:** Skittering hi-hats, hard snare hits, rhythmic complexity
- **G-Funk:** Laid-back groove, syncopated, funk-influenced
- **Emo Rap:** Trap drums + live elements, emotional space
- **Old School:** 808/909 drum machines, disco-influenced, repetitive

### 📊 **Typical BPM Ranges**

- **Trap:** 130-150 BPM (feels half-time due to beat structure)
- **Boom Bap:** 85-95 BPM (classic head-nod tempo)
- **Melodic Rap:** 125-140 BPM (modern streaming-friendly)
- **Drill:** 135-145 BPM (intense, aggressive pace)
- **Conscious Rap:** 85-95 BPM (lyrical storytelling pace)
- **Old School:** 95-115 BPM (danceable funk-influenced)

### 🎹 **Instrumentation Tips**

- **Trap:** Dark synth pads, minimal melody, atmospheric textures
- **Boom Bap:** Jazz/soul samples, piano loops, vinyl crackle
- **Melodic Rap:** Guitar + trap fusion, lush pads, emotional chords
- **Drill:** Dark piano, ominous synths, minimal warmth
- **Conscious:** Live instrumentation, organic sounds, soulful samples
- **G-Funk:** Synthesizer leads, talk-box, funky bass lines
