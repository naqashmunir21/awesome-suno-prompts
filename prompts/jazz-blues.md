## Production Tips

### 🎺 **Key Instruments by Style**

| Style | Lead Instruments | Rhythm Section | Notable Feature |
|-------|-----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Smooth Jazz** | Sax, guitar | Rhodes, soft drums | Radio-friendly polish |
| **Bebop** | Trumpet, sax | Piano, walking bass | Breakneck tempos |
| **Chicago Blues** | Electric guitar, harmonica | Shuffled drums | Gritty overdriven tone |
| **Fusion** | Electric guitar, keys | Slap bass, complex drums | Technical virtuosity |
| **Big Band** | Full horn section | Swing drums | Large ensemble |
| **Neo-Soul Jazz** | Rhodes, sax | Hip-hop drums | J Dilla beats |

### 🎤 **Vocal Approaches**

- **Blues:** Raw emotional delivery, vocal bends, authentic pain, storytelling
- **Jazz Standards:** Sophisticated phrasing, scat singing, interpretive freedom
- **Jazz-Soul:** Smooth crooning, influenced by R&B, accessible melody
- **Bebop Vocals:** Scat singing, instrumental-like phrasing, complex rhythms

### 🥁 **Drum Patterns**

- **Swing:** Ride cymbal pattern, hi-hat on 2 and 4, brushed snare
- **Blues Shuffle:** Triplet feel, backbeat emphasis, simple groove
- **Bebop:** Complex ride patterns, polyrhythmic, fast tempo
- **Fusion:** Rock-influenced, complex time signatures, technical fills
- **Smooth Jazz:** Soft brushes or light sticks, minimal intrusion
- **Neo-Soul Jazz:** Hip-hop influenced, J Dilla swing, modern groove

### 📊 **Typical BPM Ranges**

- **Smooth Jazz:** 80-110 BPM (relaxed sophisticated)
- **Bebop:** 180-260 BPM (fast virtuosic)
- **Blues:** 60-130 BPM (varies by style: slow ballads to jump blues)
- **Fusion:** 100-180 BPM (varies widely, often odd time)
- **Big Band Swing:** 130-160 BPM (danceable swing tempo)
- **Contemporary Jazz:** 70-120 BPM (modern varied approach)

### 🎚️ **Recording/Mix Approaches**

- **Smooth Jazz:** Polished radio production, compressed, pristine clarity
- **Bebop:** Natural room sound, vintage mic techniques, live feel
- **Chicago Blues:** Vintage tube amp overdrive, raw authentic sound
- **Fusion:** Clear separation for complex parts, modern production
- **Big Band:** Live ensemble recording, natural room acoustics
- **Neo-Soul Jazz:** Hip-hop production + jazz instrumentation hybrid
