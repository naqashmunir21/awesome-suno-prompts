# Show & Tell

Share your creations made with prompts from this repo. When posting, include:

- Prompt used (copy-paste)
- Model / tool & settings (e.g., Suno model, temp, length)
- A short audio link or embed (if available)
- What you changed or tuned
- Optional: before/after prompt if you optimized it

This thread highlights community successes — feel free to ask for feedback and tips.
