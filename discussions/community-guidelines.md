# Community Guidelines

Welcome — please follow these simple guidelines to keep the community constructive and friendly.

- Be respectful. No harassment or hateful content.
- Keep prompts safe and legal. Do not request or share prompts that produce private or harmful content.
- Follow formatting in `CONTRIBUTING.md` when submitting PRs.
- Use the appropriate discussion category (Prompt Requests, Show & Tell, General, Q&A).
- When posting audio or media, ensure you have rights to share it.

Moderation: maintainers may remove content that violates these rules. If you have a problem, contact the maintainers via an issue or Discord.
